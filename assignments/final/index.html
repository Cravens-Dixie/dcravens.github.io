Ride the Continental Divide

Ride the longest off-pavement route in the world.

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route (GDMBR) is Adventure Cycling's premier off-pavement cycling route, crisscrossing the Continental Divide north to south. This route is defined by the word "remote."

Ride it all or join us for a 542 mile trek starting in Roosville, MT and ending in Polaris, MT.
