Group Excursions

2018 Tour Excursions June 1st-September 15th

Come on an adventure with us as we take daily tours of exciting Bozeman bike destinations. Each tour is guided by a seasoned expert who knows how to show you a great time and make sure your experience is unforgettable. Take a look at our 2018 schedule to find the perfect excursion for you. We promise it's more fun to travel with a group!

Excusion "Sightseeing"

Excursion "Sightseeing" is a relaxed tour perfect for all ages and skill levels. We start at the Park Square at 5pm Monday and Wednesday for a 2.5-hour ride through 8 miles of trails through Bozeman State Park. This same tour runs Saturday morning at 930 am for all you weekend warriors! No maximum group size. Call the store and let us know you are coming, or just show up. It's all the same to us.

Tour fee $5. Pay by phone, pay at the store, or pay with cash at the trailhead. (Mon-Sat 10-8) 999-999-9999.

Excursion "Mountain Ride"

Excursion "Mountain Ride" is a 2-hour guided introduction to mountain biking and is a great for experienced bikers who want to learn mountain bike skills. Our guide will explain the mechanics of each type of terrain, as it comes, and is a fountain of tips for expert mountain biking. The tour meets at the Bozeman State Park 10-mile Trail parking lot on Tuesday and Thursday at 5pm, and 2pm on Saturday.

Tour fee $15. Pay by phone, pay at the store, or pay with cash at the trailhead, just make sure to call ahead! Maximum group size is 12. If you have questions about the trail or your bike, call the store during business hours (Mon-Sat 10-8) 999-999-9999.
